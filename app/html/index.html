Komatsu PC18 Mini Excavator

The PC18MR-3, the smallest digger in Komatsu's mini excavator range, is an ideal machine for any job that requires earthmoving and excavation work in small and confined spaces. It's nimble and compact, but powerful enough to work in tough and challenging conditions.

No matter what types of material you need to work with, Komatsu has a range of buckets and bucket accessories that help you ensure you can get the best from your PC18MR-3 mini digger. We work closely with Wedgelock Industries, a New Zealand-based, family owned organisation that is renowned around the world for the quality and diversity of its range of buckets and excavating solutions.

\
Tilt buckets

Size: 450mm/0.049m3

We can supply a full range of BTX tilt buckets, featuring angled cylinders for greater force, while incorporating all the advantages of Wedgelock's BCX bucket range.

General purpose buckets

Size: 930mm/0.102m3

The BDX range of buckets, suitable for mini diggers, as well as other excavator sizes, are manufactured using high quality steels, including wear resistant materials to optimise strength, weight and wear properties. As standard, they include a range of adaptors, tips and bolt-on side cutters. Optional slipper plates can also be supplied.

Draw pin couplers

Size: 450mm/0.049m3

If you need to change buckets frequently, the Draw Pin Coupler is a simple coupler developed specifically for mini excavators. It's a low-cost, easy-to-use solution that lets digger operators change attachments just by pulling a single bucket pin. Draw Pin Couplers include a lifting eye and make a perfect entry level product for mini diggers such as the PC18MR-3.
